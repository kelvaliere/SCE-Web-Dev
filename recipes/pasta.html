Pasta

A pasta with unique flair: corn and parmesan.


Ingredients

  • 4 medium ears of corn, shucked
  • ½ large yellow onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Kosher salt (such as Diamond Crystal)
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper, plus more for serving
  • 1 pound pasta of choice, such as linguine
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan (4 ounces), plus more for serving

Instructions

  1. Cut corn kernels off the ears and slice the onion and garlic.
  2. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Add onion and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add corn kernels, garlic, and black pepper. Cook for 10 minutes until corn is tender.
  5. Blend the corn mixture until smooth. Add Parmesan and stir until melted.
  6. Mix the pasta with the corn sauce. Add a little pasta water if needed to make it creamy.
  7. Serve with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.
