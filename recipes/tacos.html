Birria Tacos

Birria tacos, spicy and delicious.


Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Chopped onion and cilantro

Instructions

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper. Brown in a pot for 5 minutes.
  2. Add onion, garlic, and taco seasoning. Cover with water and simmer for 1 hour.
  3. Shred the beef when tender.
  4. Heat tortillas in a pan. Add beef and cheese, fold in half.
  5. Cook until crispy, about 2 minutes per side.
  6. Serve with chopped onion and cilantro on top.
